Our Story

Built by enthusiasts,
for enthusiasts.

Revaro exists because finding local car events shouldn't require scrolling through five different Facebook groups and three Discord servers.

The Problem

Car culture is everywhere. Finding it shouldn't be a job.

Every weekend, hundreds of meets, shows, cruises, and track days happen across the country. But they're scattered — buried in event pages, group chats, and forum posts that disappear before you even see them.

We've all missed events we would've loved. Not because they didn't exist — because nobody told us about them.

🏁
One Place for All of It

Whether it's a Sunday morning Cars & Coffee or a serious track day — if it's automotive, it belongs on Revaro.

What We Stand For

Three things we don't compromise on.

Community First

Events are posted by real people, not algorithms. Organizers get tools to reach their audience directly.

Always Free

Discovering and posting events will always be free. Car culture shouldn't have a paywall.

No BS

No ads shoved in your face, no data sold, no fake events. Just the car community, as it is.
